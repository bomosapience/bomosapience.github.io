PAYROLL ADMINISTRATION

Payroll is our dedicated service with which our clients outsource their payroll procedures and processes. It is offered in a very cost effective manner which relieves our associates of the burden from tedious administrative and routine responsibilities.

In a changing Indian business scenario this is emerging as a very important vertical. 


RECRUITMENT PROCESS OUTSOURCING (RPO) SOLUTION

RPO functions as an extension of the associate organization. We manage the A to Z process from creating job profile to on-boarding a new hire. Our dedicated executives get deployed at our associates’ realm of business operations.

We categorise our services in the following sectors: 

End-to-End RPO 

Managing the overall recruitment process.

Project Based Macro RPO 

Managing the overall recruitment process in short-term hiring goals.

Selective Micro RPO 

Managing a distinct recruitment processes within the associate organization.

Recruiter-On-Deployment 

Providing trained recruiters, skilled with our knowledge and innovations to supplement the associate organizational team.

 

BACKGROUND CHECK and VERIFICATION

This is an independent vertical which engages highly skilled and specialized executives who work in a fast and cost effective manner to help associates to avoid risk of fraud and litigations.
