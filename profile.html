COMPANY PROFILE

OUR STRENGTH
BOMO SAPIENCE is enriched by a decade of experience of its executives and a structured environment assures optimum alignment to each client’s needs, brand and culture. The organization led by industry thought leaders is dedicated to ensure optimum effectiveness by sourcing & innovative functioning in identifying and competing for right talent acquisition. Our recruitment executives leverage our vastly growing database of finest professionals to address requirements in a very cost effective manner while maintaining short deadlines. Our expert team of consultants are able to understand and enhance the profiles of the candidates, enabling them to position themselves better with prospective employers. Our knowledge of the market, customer base and ability to develop a possible list of target organisations help generate optimum results.

OUR VISION
To be one of the leading HR Solutions providers on a Pan-Asian and other larger international basis. To reach 100000 in numbers by 2019. We eye to exceed our clients’ expectations by consistently delivering services.

OUR MISSION
To serve our clients with a commitment to excellence, integrity, good ethical business practices and value added services. To build a strong mutually satisfying business relationship with our clients on a long term basis. To be recognized by our clients as the preferred man power services partner.

OUR CORE VALUES
To be driven by the core values of partnership, commitment, integrity, confidentiality, tenacity, openness, adaptability and success. We believe it is really important to invest quality time to listen to our clients’ needs, understand the aims, objectives and culture of their business. This helps us to ensure that our candidates are not just the best qualified, but also the most suitable for the role.
